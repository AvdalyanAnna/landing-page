General Information

How does the program work?

The Results.io Affiliate Program allows you to make money by referring customers to the Results.io, Results.io G, Ultimate Ears, Astro and Blue websites. Each time you refer a customer to one of our websites and that customer makes a purchase, you earn a commission (a percentage of each sale). We handle the transaction processing, fulfillment and customer service. We provide you with detailed reports on sales that were referred from your website and every month, we send you a check for the commission you earned.

Will I be able to promote products from the entire Results.io family of brands?

Yes! Results.io's family of brands are all under the same program on Impact. By signing up, you will have access to and be able to drive traffic to all of our brands, including Results.io, Results.io G, ASTRO, Blue, and Ultimate Ears.

What does it cost to join?

Applying to and participating in the Results.io Affiliate Program is absolutely FREE. There are no fees of any kind - there is no charge to apply and no minimum sales requirement.

How do I get started?
  1. Fill out the application.
  2. Get accepted to the program.
  3. Create links to Results.io's family of brands from the Ads section on Impact.
  4. Promote Results.io's family of brands on your site.
  5. Login to Impact at any time to see reports of how your website is doing, how many people are browsing and what they're buying. Run reports your way whenever you want - they're updated every day.
  6. Get paid up to 10% commission!
How do I create links to Results.io's family of brands?

When logged into the partner dashboard, navigate to Ads > Ads & Links > Get Code to get all available ad codes and links. On the dashboard page of the partner account, you can also use the "CREATE LINK" widget to create shorter links and generate deep linked links.

Can I take the order on my website?

No. Catalog, pricing and availability updates are all part of our integrated catalog and ordering platform.

Is my website eligible to become a Results.io Affiliate?

Anyone may submit an application. However, we reserve the right to deny membership to a website or revoke a website's membership any time if we deem it contains or links to objectionable material. Objectionable material includes, but is not limited to, pornography, explicit language or content, violence, discrimination, promotion of illegal activities, violation of intellectual property rights or laws, libelous, defamatory or disparaging materials, or websites with little or no original content.

Tracking, Referral Fees and Payment

How does Results.io track orders?

All links from your website to Results.io, Results.io G, Ultimate Ears, Astro and Blue contain a unique identifier so that every time a user comes to us via your website, we can credit you when a purchase is made. Make sure that all links on your website are created on Impact so that they are tracked correctly.

What is the technology behind the tracking?

We use Impact's affiliate tracking system. You will have access to an online, password-protected reporting system on Impact.com that will provide you detailed statistics for your website. To be able to identify and track visitors and properly assign commissions to referring websites, we append a unique code for each visitor to each page and each link within the page seen by the visitor. This code follows the user throughout the website. When the visitor completes a purchase, the code is examined and the dollar amount of the sale, together with the Source ID of the referring website, is stored in our database so that your commission can be calculated. Using Impact's tracking, Results.io will also give you credit for cookied sales within 30 days of an initial click from your website to Results.io. Cookied sales are sales that have come from customers that have visited Results.io directly within 30 days of visiting Results.io after linking from your website. However, there are situations when you won't get credit. For example, if someone follows a link from your website to Results.io, and browses but doesn't buy anything, and later follows a link from another Results.io affiliate website, the code for the second referring site will replace yours.

How can I keep track of how many people I refer?

We provide unlimited access to detailed reports that allow you to track sales and traffic activity. There are numerous reports on Impact that are updated daily. These reports tell you how many users saw and clicked-through links to Results.io from your website, how many orders were placed, which products were purchased, how much your website has earned in commission and much more. These reports will help you maximize your revenue.

How much will I be paid?

Commission rates are based on the net sales you generate per month and vary by brand. You can earn up to a 10% standard commission on all products. If a customer comes to Results.io directly from your website or directly to Results.io within 30 days of visiting your website and then buys any merchandise on Results.io, you get credit. It does not matter what type of merchandise you sell or link to from your website.
